Grilled Cheese Sandwich

A Classic Comfort Food

The grilled cheese sandwich is a classic comfort food that is loved by people of all ages. It's a simple dish made with bread and cheese that is grilled to perfection. The key to a great grilled cheese sandwich is to use high-quality bread and cheese, and to cook it until the cheese is melted and the bread is crispy. There are many variations of this classic dish, and you can add any fillings you like, such as bacon, tomato, or avocado.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2-3 slices of cheese (Cheddar, Swiss, or any cheese of your choice)
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Directions

  1. Preheat a pan over medium heat.
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place the cheese between the slices of bread, with the buttered sides facing out.
  4. Place the sandwich in the preheated pan and cook until the bottom slice of bread is golden brown, about 2-3 minutes.
  5. Flip the sandwich and cook until the other side is golden brown and the cheese is melted, about 2-3 minutes.
  6. Remove the sandwich from the pan and let it cool for a minute or two before serving.
